ABOUT

What is Hello Michu?

Hello Michu (헬로미추) is a community-run tourism business based in Michuhol-gu, Incheon.
We translate the history and nature of Michuhol — the legend of Soseono, Munhak Mountain, tidal flats, and neighborhood streetscapes — into watercolor coloring experiences.
We invite you to slow down, pick up a brush, and rediscover this city one colour at a time.
